Why Virginia Buildings Need It

Built for the Queen City of the Range

Virginia's commercial buildings sit on the Mesabi Iron Range, where mining-country dust, shade, and long winters leave downtown storefronts and facades dulled fast.

Buildings here pick up fine mining-country dust and Northern Minnesota damp. That grime and moisture cling to facades and shaded walls, giving algae and mold what they need to take hold — leaving the downtown storefronts customers see first looking tired.

Add the Range's long, hard winters and seasonal buildup, and growth accumulates until buildings look dated. Our commercial soft washing clears it gently and protects your image, with crews scheduled around your hours.
