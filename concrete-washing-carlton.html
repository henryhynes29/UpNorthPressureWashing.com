Why Carlton Concrete Gets Dirty

Built for the Jay Cooke Gateway

Carlton concrete takes on road salt, sand, river-gorge damp, and freeze-thaw all year. Our hot water pressure washing is built to handle exactly those conditions.

Between winter salt and sand and the damp air off the St. Louis River gorge, Carlton driveways and walks pick up grime, algae, and rust fast. Cold water just smears oil around; hot water breaks it down for a clean, even finish.

Add Carlton's long winters and repeated freeze-thaw, and untreated stains dig deeper into the slab each year. Our heated equipment lifts it all out — restoring sharp, safe concrete that holds up to the next Northern Minnesota season.
