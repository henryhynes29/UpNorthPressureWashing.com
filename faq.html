Frequently Asked Questions

What is soft washing?

Soft washing uses low pressure and professional cleaning solutions to safely remove algae, mold, and grime from siding and roofs — without the damage risk of high-pressure washing.

Do you serve my city?

We serve Duluth, Hermantown, Proctor, Cloquet, Superior, Two Harbors, Esko, Carlton County, the North Shore, and the Iron Range. See all service areas.

What's the difference between soft washing and pressure washing?

Soft washing is for delicate surfaces like siding and roofs. Hot water pressure washing is for concrete, driveways, and heavy grease or salt buildup.

Are you insured?

Yes — we carry $1M+ liability insurance and are fully insured for residential and commercial work.

Do you offer a guarantee?

Soft washes include a 1-year growth-free guarantee. If organic growth returns within 12 months, we retreat at no charge.

Is roof cleaning safe for my shingles?

Yes — we use low-pressure soft washing recommended by shingle manufacturers. Never high PSI, which strips granules and voids warranties. Ideal for Duluth, the North Shore, and Iron Range homes with algae and moss.

What is gutter cleaning vs. gutter guard installation?

We clean existing gutters, fascia, and soffits — removing tiger stripes, algae, and buildup. We do not install guards; we restore the roofline you already have.

Best time for roof cleaning in Minnesota?

Late spring through early fall is ideal — after pollen season and before heavy ice. Many homeowners schedule roof and gutter cleaning together in one visit.
