How salt damages Moose Lake property

Chlorides penetrate porous concrete and wick into masonry. Repeated freeze-thaw cycles expand micro-cracks until surfaces flake and pit.

Spring is the critical window

Schedule salt removal as soon as snow melts — before summer heat bakes residue into permanent stains on Moose Lake flatwork.

Garage interiors and aprons

Vehicles drip brine onto aprons all season. Hot-water degreasing restores the transition zone between street and home.

Don't forget the siding splash zone

Salt-laden splash up to 24 inches off grade discolors vinyl and wood. Soft washing clears it before oxidation starts.
